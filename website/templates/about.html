About Our Employee Tracking System

Welcome to our Employee Sign-in & Sign-out System! Our goal is to provide a simple and efficient way for employees to track their working hours.

🚀 What We Offer

Our system is designed to help organizations:

  • Track employee attendance effortlessly.
  • Provide secure login and authentication.
  • Allow admins to monitor work hours in real-time.

👨‍💻 Who We Are

We are a team of developers passionate about creating tools that improve workplace efficiency. Our system is built with **Flask**, **SQLite**, and **Flask-Login** for secure user authentication.
